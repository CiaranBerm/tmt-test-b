Trail Making Test
Parts A & B

A two-part cognitive assessment. Part A measures motor speed; Part B measures cognitive flexibility. The difference between them is your set-shifting score.

Takes about 3–5 minutes. Complete both parts back-to-back. Results are anonymous — a unique device ID links your pre and post scores for comparison.
